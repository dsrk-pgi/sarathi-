Privacy Policy

Introduction

Sarathi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Sarathi stores all your data locally on your device only. We collect and store:

  • Documents: Names, numbers, and expiry dates of your documents (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Bills: Bill details, amounts, and due dates
  • Health Data: Medicine names, dosages, and reminder times
  • Preferences: Language selection and app settings

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT upload your data to any servers
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT track your usage
  • We do NOT use analytics or advertising

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally using AsyncStorage on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display your documents, bills, and health information
  • Send local notifications for reminders
  • Provide bilingual interface based on your preference

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No internet connection required for core features
  • Data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device lock/encryption

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is visible within the app
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all data
  • Export: Currently not available (future feature)

Children's Privacy

Sarathi does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Third-Party Services

Sarathi uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo: For app framework (https://expo.dev/privacy)
  • React Native: For mobile development

These services may collect anonymous usage data as per their own privacy policies.

Permissions

Sarathi requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send medicine and bill reminders
  • Microphone: (Future) For voice assistant feature

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@sarathi.app
  • GitHub: github.com/dsrk-pgi/sarathi-

Consent

By using Sarathi, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
